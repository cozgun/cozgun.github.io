Deadline Followup & Reminder Tool

Concerned about missing important deadlines ? Not anymore !

Every morning tool lets you and your team know of what needs to be done on that day in an e-mail. In another e-mail it sends the tasks should be done in five days ahead.

If anyone in team misses a date, other team members will be awake of this and things can be sorted out before deadline without any incidents.

Tool will be sending morning e-mails until someone in team opens the app marks the job done.

Another functionality is making sure of tasks (where required) are checked by four eyes. After one team member marks the job as done, tool expects another team member to control and marked it as "checked". Tool keep sending morning e-mails until four eyes check completed.

Four eyes check records can be retrived later in report format for audit purposes.

Add-in: Smart Outlook Assistant - SOA

Need to send same e-mail everyday/week/month ?
Have files to check at a specific time of day?
Then I can be your assistant.
Advantages:

You do not need to think about the recurring e-mail messages you need to send daily/weekly/monthly. SOA will take care of them as long as your Outlook remains open.

By doing so, SOA will save you time and minimize the risk of missing an e-mail.

But my favorite usage of SOA is this: Everyday I need to open & check many files which includes validation results, report submission proofs or automated system outputs. These need to be done in specific times of day and should not be missed. SOA checks the relevant directories for me at these specific times and sends e-mail to me and my teammates with attachment or in other case with a warning like -attachment not found-.
